## Squads

### OPS — Platform, Engineering Tooling & Operations
Declarations, taxes, periodic routines, platform services, income reporting, day-to-day bugs & tasks.

### Entry Journey — Client Onboarding
Company registration, post-sale, onboarding, conversational AI, corporate structure, forms & app.

### FinGrowth — Benefits, Finance, MEI & Growth
Multi-benefits, client financials, Agilize financials, MEI, Company Hero, Growth.

### MarIA & ClaudIA — Applied AI
Generative AI, RAG, Chat & WhatsApp, email & phone, ticket reduction, client self-service.

### Structural Areas
- **InfraSecGov** — AWS & cloud, access management, security & governance
- **Data** — Data lake, analytics, business intelligence
- **Engineering** — Quality & standards, cross-squad technical leadership

### Cross-squad (Transversals)
Principal engineer, frontend tech lead, and product designers available across all squads.

## Rituals

**STT Day — Stock killers**: Bi-weekly (2nd and last Thursday of the month). Seniors from multiple squads focus on reducing the backlog of bugs and tasks. Temporary until accounting stabilization.

**OPS Root Cause rotation**: Senior rotation, 1 day/week per person, focused on root cause analysis. Israel (Mon), Carlos (Tue), Calison (Wed), Ernesto (Thu), Adriano (Fri).
